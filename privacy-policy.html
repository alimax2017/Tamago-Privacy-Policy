Privacy Policy - Tamago

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Tamago ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use the Tamago mobile application ("App").

2. Data Collection and Use

2.1 Personal Data

We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location) through the App.

2.2 App Content Data (Stored Locally)

Task and project information you create in Tamago is stored locally on your device to provide the App’s core features.

2.3 Analytics

Tamago does not use analytics services to track user behavior.

2.4 Crash Reporting

Tamago does not use third-party crash reporting services.

2.5 Advertising

Tamago does not display ads and does not use advertising SDKs.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Permissions

Tamago only requests permissions required for app functionality. If future versions request additional permissions, this policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since data is stored locally on your device, you can delete app data at any time by:

  • Clearing app storage in your device settings, or
  • Uninstalling the App.

6. Children’s Privacy

Tamago is not designed to knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will address the issue promptly.

7. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data handled by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

8. International Users

Because Tamago primarily stores data locally on-device, cross-border data transfers are generally not required.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
